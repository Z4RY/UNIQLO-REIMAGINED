What isLifeWear?
magazine

LifeWear is clothing designed to make everyone's life better.

It is a simple, high-quality, everyday clothing with a practical sense of beauty ingenious in detail,thought with life's needs in mind, and always evolving.

As a way of helping people all over the world to explore the specifies of this clothing and the concepts behind its production,
we founded LifeWear magazine.

Our Values.

"It's time for action, in the name of world peace." Major figures who share this vision with UNIQLO have volunteered to designed T-shirts embodying their peaceful wishes.

All proceeds will be denotedd to international organizations supporting those affected by violence, discrimination,armed conflict, and poverty. The PEACE FOR ALL project is powered by each and every one you who weres these T-shirt.

Our wish is for a world in which all people can feel safe in their daily lives.
